# Business Continuity Contract

## Purpose

This contract defines business continuity expectations during backup, restore, or disaster recovery events.

## Required Continuity Controls

- incident commander is assigned
- technical lead is assigned
- privacy owner is assigned
- communications owner is assigned
- escalation matrix is available
- support communication path is available
- learner data protection remains active
- POPIA data subject rights are triaged during outage
- manual workaround is documented where feasible
- post-incident review is required

## Boundary

This contract records business continuity readiness. It does not execute incident response.
