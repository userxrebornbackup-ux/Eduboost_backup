# GA Graduation Boundary Contract

## Required GA Boundary Controls

- beta exit criteria must be reviewed
- production launch approval must be separate
- live billing must remain disabled until approved
- external/manual approvals must be complete
- critical known issues must be zero or explicitly blocked
- critical post-baseline risks must block GA
- support and operations readiness must be reviewed
- privacy and security review must be complete where applicable

## Boundary

This contract records GA graduation boundaries. It does not approve GA or production launch.
