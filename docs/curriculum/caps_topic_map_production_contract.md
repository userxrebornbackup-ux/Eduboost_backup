# CAPS Topic Map Production Contract

## Required CAPS map fields

- phase
- grade
- subject
- term
- topic
- subtopic
- prerequisites
- assessment standards

## Required CAPS controls

- generated content references a valid CAPS topic
- full CAPS coverage claims are prevented until coverage is validated
- curriculum coverage dashboard remains post-baseline evidence
- topics without lessons remain detectable after baseline
- topics without diagnostics remain detectable after baseline
- topics without practice questions remain detectable after baseline
- topics without quality-reviewed content remain detectable after baseline
- alignment confidence score is required per lesson
- teacher-facing CAPS coverage export remains post-baseline evidence
- curriculum maps are versioned after baseline

## Boundary

This repository-side CAPS topic map contract records canonical map structure and validation evidence only. It does not assert national curriculum completeness.
