# Transparent product roadmap

## Now: production hardening and private alpha

- Keep launch scope narrow.
- Validate consent, diagnostics, lessons, parent reporting, support, monitoring, and backup/restore.
- Use synthetic or explicitly consented users only.

## Next: trustworthy beta

- Public beta label.
- Feedback/report issue button.
- Content correction workflow.
- Human review queue for content and erasure-sensitive cases.
- Teacher/educator review of priority CAPS topics.

## Later: differentiated South African learning platform

- CAPS-aligned learning graph.
- Parent co-pilot with privacy controls.
- Teacher/classroom mode after workflow validation.
- Offline/low-data lesson packs.
- Ethical learning analytics with explainable recommendations.
- Sponsored learner and NGO/community impact reporting.
