# Future differentiation strategy

EduBoost should differentiate through trust, local relevance, and operational restraint rather than generic AI novelty.

## Differentiators

1. South African CAPS learning graph.
2. POPIA-first learner data architecture.
3. Visible trust labels for generated content.
4. Parent co-pilot focused on practical 10-minute support.
5. Low-data/offline resilience.
6. Ethical analytics using constructive, explainable recommendations.
7. Sponsored learner/community impact model.

## Anti-differentiators to avoid

- Claiming complete CAPS coverage too early.
- Treating AI output as automatically correct.
- Compulsive streak mechanics.
- Leaderboards that shame learners.
- Collecting unnecessary learner data because it is technically easy.
