# Runbook: Privacy Incident

## Detection

- confirm privacy signal
- preserve evidence
- identify data class

## Triage

- classify severity
- notify privacy owner
- identify affected users

## Mitigation

- disable affected path
- restrict access
- stop data flow

## Recovery

- verify access controls
- confirm data subject rights path

## Verification

- confirm no ongoing exposure
- record evidence

## Rollback Criteria

- uncertain containment
- continued exposure
- legal notification needed

## Boundary

This runbook records privacy incident response readiness. It does not execute legal or privacy notification.
