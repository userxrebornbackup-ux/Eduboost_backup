# ADR-020: Final Release-Blocker Checklist

## Status

Accepted for repository-side production-readiness evidence.

## Decision

EduBoost V2 will use a final release-blocker checklist to consolidate repository-side production-readiness evidence, release-blocker ownership, closure evidence, waiver rules, external/manual dependency boundaries, launch authority, and final go/no-go decision discipline.

## Required Controls

- final release-blocker checklist is required
- release-blocker owner assignment is required
- closure evidence is required
- waiver policy is required
- release-blocker severity cannot be waived by default
- external/manual dependency boundary is required
- launch authority is required
- final go/no-go decision is required
- known issues review is required
- rollback review is required
- support review is required
- privacy/security review is required

## Boundary

This ADR records final release-blocker checklist evidence. It does not approve external settings, legal signoff, security signoff, deployment, beta launch, general availability, or production launch.
